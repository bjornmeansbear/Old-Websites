As we come across Floyd, he is already being chased by that lovable two dimensional robot of doom, Tor... As the chase continues, Floyd's silly human, yet immortal body begins to tire, and Tor closes the gap slowly... Floyd only hopes that he can find somewhere to hide or lose Tor shortly before he has to suffer the horrible pains of dying only to live again...

 

 

As the chase wound down to it's end, Tor almost had Floyd in his grasp... Fortunately for Floyd however, he had run to his friend, Ghetto Stan's place of work and residence. Floyd knew he could fight off Tor from there, at least along with the help of his friends...
