PORK

Pork Gyvorski is the younger brother or Sven. Being a senior in high school still, Sven uses every chance he gets to go meet Pork at school in hopes of landing a high school girl for a date or two. Pork is also responsible to page and call Sven every once in a while and pretend to be one of Sven's "girlfriends..." The saddest part is that Pork actually does all these things so that he can hang out with Sven, Floyd and Afro-Boy... poor lost soul...
