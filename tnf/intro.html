Ahh Yes, the city. Where our tale unfolds. Down there in the busy streets, subways and sidewalks trouble has been brewing for quite some time. Down below the misadventures of our hero Floyd unfold as Tor tries to kill and maim him...

 

 

However, this isn't where this story really started. Our story really starts somewhere else, and that somewhere is the foul, sweaty inferno that is hell...

 

It started with the Dark lord of the underworld himself, Lucifer, Beelzebub, whatever, sending his two dimensional robot of destruction off to destroy our hero, Floyd...
