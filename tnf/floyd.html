FLOYD

Floyd is the unsuspecting victim of Satan's wrath. Why this is the case is still unclear to most everyone. Floyd cannot die but can unfortunately feel pain. This is perhaps why Satan has chosen Floyd as the target of his two dimensional robot of destruction Tor, for the sheer pleasure of watching someone indefinitely suffer... whatever, anyway, Floyd is our unfortunate hero, and he and his friends are always on the look out for Tor, Robo-Floyd and any other of Satan's minions
