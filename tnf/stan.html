GHETTO STAN

Ghetto Stan is the proud owner of Ghetto Stan's House of Everything, the local hang out spot for Floyd and his buddies. Stan was once a great, rapping, short-order cook, but now just runs his place... Ghetto Stan's House of Everything is basically a pawn, consignment and stolen goods shop, along with hardware and a short order restaurant attached... Stan's specialty is copying those damn keys that say "do not copy" on them...
