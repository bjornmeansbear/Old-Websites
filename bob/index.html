Welcome to the Brick Oven Bakery. Located in lovely Northfield, Minnesota, the Brick Oven Bakery produces handcrafted breads lovingly made with no preservatives. We offer a wide variety of breads, rolls, stickie buns, scones and cookies. We also offer specialty items, like canapés, available by order.


Join our e-mail list by sending us an email with “Add me to your mailing list” in the subject line. You will get our newsletters by email, and get our monthly “Brick Oven Baking Word of the Month” which entitles you to a free punch on your Baker’s Dozen card each month.
