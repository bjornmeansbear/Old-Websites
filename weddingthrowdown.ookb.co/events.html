wedding weekend:

Friday October 17th 6-10 pm

Pig Roast & Megashow

    • 14221 Hawthorn Road
    • Lemont, IL 60439
    • directions & map
  • Those of you who know Chris know he likes to (nick)name things. Everyone is invited to “Megashow,” our amped-up version of a rehearsal dinner which will feature roasted pig, s’mores, and live music by Chris, his brother Jason, and other musician friends like Scotland Yard Gospel Choir and Kristian Bjornard. The pig roast will be a casual affair at the Lemont, IL home of Chris’s parents. Shuttle service will be available to and from downtown Chicago and parking is abundant.

Saturday October 18th 12-3 pm

Formal Reception

    • Tru
    • 676 North Saint Clair Street
    • Chicago, IL 60611
    • directions & map
  • Our formal reception will be held at Tru in downtown Chicago. Meredith worked as a doula for Tru’s executive pastry chef and adores her and her family. Tru is in our opinion (and in opinions that may matter more than ours like those of Phil Vettel of the Chicago Tribune) quite possibly the best restaurant in Chicago. We’ll don our wedding attire, snack on sweet and savory treats, in lieu of a sit-down meal, and sip bubbly (a Meredith favorite) and other exciting liquids (all favorites of Chris, the drinkaholic). We request no kids under 10 at this party. Parking downtown can be tricky. Valet at Tru will be available.

Saturday, October 18th 9pm-2am

Throwdown IX

    • Fizz Bar & Grill
    • 3220 North Lincoln Ave
    • Chicago, Il 60657
    • directions & map
  • This party will mark the 8th annual throwdown hosted by Chris. Meredith has attended the last five, and many of you have throwndown with us in the past. Our close friend and exceptional DJ Mike LaRosa (supa.com) will spin; we’ll (not chicken) dance and enjoy an open bar until the wee hours of the morning. The Throwdown will be held at Fizz Bar & Grill, in their upstairs party room. Attendees under 21 are welcome but will only be admitted with their parents. Fizz has free parking in the city lot on the 1600 block of Melrose just north and west of the establishment.
