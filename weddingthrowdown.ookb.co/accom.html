Travelling to and Staying in Chicago

Chicago Airports

Chicago has two major airports: O’Hare (ORD) and Midway (MDW). The truth is, flying into Chicago is a pick-your-poison sort of situation. O’Hare is infamous for flight delays (Chicagoans like to blame the weather rather than the infrastructure) while Midway was once accurately described by an out-of-town friend as, “Disneyland without the rides” (long lines, hoards of families, and miserable kids). The upside of Chicago’s airports is that they are both linked to the city by public transportation. O’Hare is on the blue line and is approximately a 40 minute ride to downtown while the ride from Midway to downtown on the orange line should take about 35 minutes. www.ohare.com or midway information.
