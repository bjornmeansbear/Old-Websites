Wedding Registry

We decided to register on a site called My Registry.

On My Registry, we were able to register for any item available on the interweb! When you click “buy gift”, the site will prompt you to enter your email address and then automatically link you to the website where we selected the item. Though you are making the purchase on a different website, My Registry will maintain a header on the new site which will list our shipping address if you need it. We tried to find the best bargains, but you could (if you were highly motivated) take the information from our link and procure the item at another site or at a physical store. Once you make a purchase, My Registry will direct you back to their site where you are prompted to mark the item as fulfilled or not. We hope this foray into new internet technology will not prove too difficult.
